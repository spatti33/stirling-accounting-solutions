Who we are

Stirling Accounting Solutions was founded on the need to provide high level accurate accounting and bookkeeping services to small and medium sized companies. After having worked in various industries ranging from health care, legal, tech start-up, and advertising, one thing became apparent: Small and medium sized companies needed personalized, dedicated, and experienced accounting services. Employee turnover, lack of training, and inconsistant bookkeeping methods have a huge impact on any company but more so on small and medium sized businesses. Anything that takes your focus away from running your business is costly.

With over 20 years of experience, Lesley has a keen sense of what small and medium sized businesses need to become financially organized and position themselves to accelerate growth. Whether your financial needs are small, ranging from accounts payable to data entry, or more involved, such as software training or establishing your personalized accounting programs, Stirling Accounting Solutions will partner with you as you grow.

To be the most trusted and respected accounting and bookkeeping resource by providing innovative and practical services that contribute to clients success.
