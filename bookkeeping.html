bookkeeping

full service for a worry free and efficient process from start to finish

Accurate bookkeeping provides all vital information regarding your business, which allows you to make the best decisions for growth. It helps by presenting past financial performance of your company and can help you stategize for the future. With organized and well-managed bookkeeping from Stirling Accounting Solutions, your business can closely monitor it's financial capabilities and accelerate growth through informed decisions.

  • Income/Expense Recording
  • Invoicing
  • A/R & Cash Receipts
  • Payroll Management
  • Quickbooks® Set-up
  • Bank Deposits
  • Accounts Payable Management
  • Day to Day Transactions
  • Credit Card Reconciliation
  • Month-End Close
  • Monthly Reconciliations
  • 1099 Preparation and Filing
